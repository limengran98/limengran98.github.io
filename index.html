Mengran Li

Ph.D. Student in Control Science & Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University

1. Personal Profile

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Engineering at the School of Intelligent Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, focusing on Control Science and Engineering. Before this, I obtained a Master's degree in Control Science and Engineering from Beijing University of Technology. My main research interests include the application of network science in social media, heterogeneous graph learning, hypergraph learning, temporal modeling, traffic big data, and bioinformatics. I am advised by Dr. Ronghui Zhang at Sun Yat-sen University and Dr. Yong Zhang at Beijing University of Technology. As the first author, I have published over 10 high-level papers in top-tier journals, including IEEE Transactions and ACM Transactions.
